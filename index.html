The Chef? Vinod Kumar Saini himself

Chef Vinod Saini, a name synonymous for creative excellence with over 30 years of experience in fine dining restaurants and luxury hotels in India and Canada and currently the Head Master Chef Indian at Jamavar, The Leela Palace New Delhi. Chef Vinod began his career with The Oberoi group of hotels in India. Started working at their flagship hotel in New Delhi in 1989, with stints in French restaurant - La Rochelle, all day dining and the signature Fine Dine Indian restaurant “Kandahar”. He assisted the Chef in ensuring high guest satisfaction and maintaining “Kandahar” as one of the best Indian restaurants in the city.

After 15 years with The Oberoi Group, he moved to Canada for opening fine dining Indian restaurants in Toronto and Vancouver in 2005. Post his international exposure of four years in, he returned to India in 2009 and joined Marut Sikka’s celebrated “Kainoosh”, a contemporary and classic Indian restaurant, as the Executive Sous Chef.

An expert of the Indian Cuisine, he has been heading the kitchen at Jamavar since its inception which serves royal Indian cuisine at The Leela Palace New Delhi, as the Indian Master Chef. From being accorded as ‘Best Indian Chef’ by Food and Nightlife, while he was the Chef de cuisine at Jamavar in May 2012 to receiving Best North Indian Restaurant in Five-Star Hotel for Jamavar at BBC Good Food Awards 2019, Chef Saini has spearheaded the restaurants’ kitchen making it the one of the preferred destination to enjoy Indian culinary fare in the city.

Chef Vinod has also been recognized by experts for his modern interpretation of the dishes from the bygone era. A culinary story teller, he draws his inspiration from the diverse Indian culture that imparts his dishes a distinctive character. He also specializes in curating exclusive cooking classes and interactive food demonstrations for guests, which take them on an indulgent journey to discover the magical history of Indian spices and their origins. Chef Vinod actively contributes to the on board menu of world renowned Lufthansa Airlines, and has also been appointed as their ‘Star Chef’ - Consultant Chef, for first and business class. He was also one of the judges on the panel of Lufthansa for their concluded ‘Cook and Fly Contest’.

His core strengths are excelling at innovative Indian food, staying within the boundaries of Indian flavors and retaining the authenticity of the cuisine. He has excellent kitchen organization skills, employee management and development, food quality monitoring, cost control and HACCP. In his free time, Vinod loves to listen to music and enjoy old retro hits.
